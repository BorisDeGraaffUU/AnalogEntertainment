About Analog Entertainment and Paul's Quest

Analog Entertainment is a project group for the University of Utrecht. For our project we made a Unity game called "Paul's Quest"

In our game, which is a blend of the retro game 'Zelda' and the newer 'Beyond two soulds' the player goes on a quest to reunite Pauls physical and his magical force.

Puzzles and combat mechanics fill this amazing 2 - player co-op game and guarentee for hours and hours of fun.
